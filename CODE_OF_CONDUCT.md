# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, inclusive, and
constructive for researchers, engineers, reviewers, and collaborators.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and precise language.
- Critique ideas, code, and evidence rather than people.
- Respect different levels of domain expertise.
- Be careful with claims in scientific and regulated contexts.
- Give and receive feedback in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Deliberate intimidation or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Misrepresenting results, validation status, or affiliations.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict
participation when behavior harms the project or its community.
